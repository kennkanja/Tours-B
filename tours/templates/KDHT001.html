5 Days Tanzania Lodge or Camping Safari


5 Day Tanzania Wildlife Lodge Safari to Tarangire, Serengeti, Ngorongoro and Lake Eyasi


5 Day Tanzania Wildlife Camping Safari to Tarangire, Serengeti, Ngorongoro and Lake Eyasi


DAY 1: Arusha - Tarangire National Park

You will depart for Tarangire National Park for a game drive in this very diverse park with picnic lunch boxes, Tarangire is well known for its large herds of Elephant population. You will proceed with the game drive until late afternoon when you will drive to your lodge or campsite for dinner & overnight.


DAY 2: Tarangire - Serengeti National Park

Early after breakfast you will drive to Serengeti National Park with picnic lunch boxes. You will have a game drive on route via the Olduvai Gorge until late in the afternoon when you will proceed to your lodge or campsite for dinner & overnight.


DAY 3: Serengeti - Ngorongoro Crater


You will have an early in the morning game drive at the Serengeti National Park and after breakfast you will proceed to the Ngorongoro Crater with picnic lunch boxes. You will have an evening game drive at the crater before you proceed to your lodge or campsite for dinner & overnight.


DAY 4: Ngorongoro Crater - Lake Eyasi

Early after breakfast you will have a full day game drive at the crater with picnic lunch boxes. Late in the afternoon you will drive to Lake Eyasi and proceed to the Lake Eyasi Campsite for dinner and overnight.


DAY 5: Lake Eyasi -Arusha

Early after breakfast you will visit the Bushmen Village to see their daily activities and you can have an optional 3-4 hours hunting tour with the Bushmen. You will also be able to try local fishing methods before you drive back to Arusha for overnight.
