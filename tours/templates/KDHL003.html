4 Days Masai Mara and Lake Nakuru Lodge safari



4 Days Lake Nakuru & Masai Mara Lodge Safari in Kenya Safari to Lake Nakuru National Park & Masai Mara National Reserve staying at Sarova Lion Hill Lodge/Lake Nakuru Lodge and Sarova Mara Camp/ Keekorok lodge, Mara Serena Game Lodge: Luxury Lodge Safaris Masai Mara and Lake Nakuru Kenya Lodge Safaris



4 Days Lake Nakuru National Park and Masai Mara lodge Safari


DAY 1: Nairobi - Masai Mara

8.00hrs: Drive through large Kikuyu farmlands to the edge of the Rift Valley. Descend the escarpment and drive via Narok to Masai Mara Game Reserve arriving in time for lunch. Lions in Masai Mara. The Masai Mara adjoins Tanzania & Serengeti National Park . Masai Mara is famous for its Black-manned and yearly migrations of wildebeest and zebra. Elephant, buffalo, rhino, gazelle, leopards, giraffe are also found here. Dinner and overnight at lodge


DAY 2: Masai Mara


A full day spent viewing and exploring this spectacular National park. All the big Five can be seen. Masai Mara is also famous for its bird life. Meals and overnight at the Lodge.


DAY 3: Lake Nakuru


After breakfast, check out and proceed to Nakuru to arrive in time for lunch. Game viewing drive at 1600hrs. Lake Nakuru National Park is located in the Rift Valley 1750 meters above sea level, Lake Nakuru is known as an ornithological paradise by birdwatchers because the lake is world famous as the location of the greatest bird spectacle on earth which boasts any where between one and two million lesser and greater pink flamingos that feed on the abundant algae thriving in the lakes warm waters.


It is one of the few parks that have been specifically established (Gazetted) for the protection of birds specifically flamingoes, more than 450 species of birds have been recorded here. The park has also been established for the protection of endangered species such as the rhino and is also home to Columbus monkeys, Leopards, Giraffes, and a variety of plain antelope. Lake Nakuru is famous for its Pink Flamingoes and many other types of birds. There are also a number of animals in this park. Dinner and overnight at a Sarova Lion Hill or Lake Nakuru Lodge


DAY 4: Lake Nakuru - Nairobi

After breakfast spend the morning watching and photographing birds and wild animals in the park. Depart hotel after lunch and leave for Nairobi stopping for panoramic views of the Great Rift Valley en-route. Arrive at Nairobi in the afternoon.
